202 /*
203  HQ - quarantined households

205  AC - Non-quarantined working adult cases absent thru sickness.
206  AA - Absent working adults who are caring for sick children (only assigned if no non-working, quarantine or sick adults available).
207  ACS - Children below care requirement cut-off age who are absent (sick or quarantined)
208 
209  APC - Non-quarantined working adult cases absent due to closure of their workplace (excl teachers).
210  APA - Absent working adults who are caring for children at home due to school closure (only assigned if no non-working, quarantine or sick adults available).
211  ACS - Children below care requirement cut-off age who are absent due to school closure
212 
213
